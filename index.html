Timeline

August 1993

September 2000 - June 2010

Got Bachelor degree in Economics in Financial University under the Government of the Russian Federation. During the study was working as intern in Procurement Department in Gazprom Neft PJSC.

I was not involved in any decisions around product and most of my work day was fixing various types of bugs.

Project was written mostly in Vanilla JS with Backbone.js library with marionette.js framework on Frontend side and Node.js on Backend side.

November 2019 - September 2020

Got a job at Wrike, Inc! It is a corporate online collaboration and project management cloud base service. It allows users to plan projects, prioritize tasks, track their progress schedule and interact with the team.

Worked on dashboard views representation and filtering features. We followed Agile methodology with transparent goals and soft deadlines. All new features were developed as Micro Frontends. I was happy to take part in one of them that greatly improves user experience.
